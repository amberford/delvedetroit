Grand Circus

Grand Circus Park is the centerpiece of Augustus Woodward's plan for the reconstruction of Detroit after the Fire of 1805. The half-circle park's perimeter features many important landmarks, including the Detroit Opera House and Comerica Park. (Wikipedia)
